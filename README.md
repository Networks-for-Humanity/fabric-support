# fabric-support

A unified support channel for network participants across all **NFH Fabric** products. If you're running into an issue, have a question, or need help getting something to work — you're in the right place.

---

## How to Get Support

Getting support is straightforward: **open an issue** in this repository.

When creating your issue, you'll be asked to select:

1. **The Fabric product** you need help with
2. **The network** you are a participant in

That's it. Our team will triage and respond as quickly as we can.

> **Don't have a GitHub account?** You'll need one to open an issue. It's free and takes under a minute to set up at [github.com](https://github.com).

| Product | Description |

| **DISCOVRY** | Discovery layer for finding providers and services on the network |

---

## Opening an Issue

1. Go to the [**Issues**](../../issues) tab
2. Click **New Issue**
3. Choose the template that matches your product
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Network** you are a participant in
   - **What you were trying to do**
   - **What happened** (error messages, screenshots, logs are very helpful)
   - **Steps to reproduce**, if applicable
5. Submit — you're done

The more context you provide upfront, the faster we can help.

---

## Before You Open an Issue

To help keep the queue clean and get to resolutions faster, please take a quick look at:

- [Open issues](../../issues) — someone may have already reported the same thing
- [Closed issues](../../issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aclosed) — it may already be resolved

---

## What to Expect

- Issues are reviewed on a best-effort basis
- Complex or environment-specific issues may require back-and-forth in the thread
- Feature requests and suggestions are also welcome — label them accordingly when filing
